VUOTO IS A MULTIDISCIPLINARY DESIGN AND ARTISTIC COLLECTIVE
BORN IN MILANO IN 2023

Born in Milan, Vuoto Collettivo is a dynamic fusion of design and artistry, rooted in an insatiable quest that transcends the perceptible.

Comprising 17 diverse individuals, this collective represents a mosaic of talents and perspectives, converging to explore realms beyond conventional boundaries.

With Milan as its backdrop, Vuoto Collettivo embarks on a journey of innovation, challenging conventional norms and redefining creative expression.

With each member bringing a unique voice to the table, the collective thrives on collaboration, pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the realms of design and art.
